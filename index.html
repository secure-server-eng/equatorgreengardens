Welcome to Green Gardens & Resort

Where luxury meets adventure right at the heart of the equator. Nestled in the serene landscapes of Mogotio, Kenya, our resort offers a unique blend of comfort and excitement. Whether you're looking to unwind or seek thrills, we have something for everyone. Indulge in our world-class services, including delectable food and refreshing drinks, all while soaking in the breathtaking views. For the adventure enthusiasts, we offer a range of activities such as camping, swimming, ziplining, and rope climbing. That's just the beginning, there's so much more to explore and enjoy! Come experience the perfect getaway at Equator Green Gardens & Resort, where unforgettable memories are made.
